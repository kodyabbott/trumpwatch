Trump Clock - Support

How It Works

Trump Clock periodically checks whether Donald J. Trump is the current President of the United States using publicly available data from Wikidata. If he is no longer in office before the end of his term, the app sends you a notification.

Notifications

Make sure notifications are enabled for Trump Clock in your device settings. The app needs notification permission to alert you.

Background Refresh

For the app to check automatically, Background App Refresh must be enabled. Go to Settings > General > Background App Refresh and make sure Trump Clock is on.
