Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Overview

Trump Clock does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

The app does not collect any personal information. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising.

Network Requests

The app queries Wikidata, a public database, to check publicly available government data. No user information is sent in these requests.

Local Storage

The app stores a small amount of data on your device using standard system preferences to remember its state. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to kody@kodyabbott.com.
